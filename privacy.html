Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

InstaAutomate collects the following information:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and profile details you provide during registration
  • Instagram data: Instagram account username, profile picture, follower count, and access tokens (encrypted)
  • Conversation data: Direct messages and comments you receive through Instagram
  • Usage data: Analytics about how you use our service
  • Payment information: Razorpay customer ID and subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Instagram automation services
  • Send automated replies to your Instagram messages and comments
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates

3. Instagram Data Storage

We store Instagram data as follows:

  • Access tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Messages and conversations are stored securely in our database
  • We only store data necessary to provide our automation services
  • We do not sell or share your Instagram data with third parties

4. AI Integration

When you use AI-powered replies:

  • Conversation history is sent to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini)
  • We use your own API key - we do not have access to your AI account
  • You can disable AI at any time in your settings

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies on all database tables
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of AI features

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account, all data including Instagram tokens, messages, and automation flows is permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and storage
  • Razorpay: Payment processing
  • Meta/Instagram: OAuth and Graph API
  • OpenAI/Anthropic/Gemini: AI services (when enabled by you)

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@instaautomate.com
